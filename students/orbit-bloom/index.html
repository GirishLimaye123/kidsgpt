Class 1 guide

Start with a human, not just an idea.

Ask your human

  • What kinds of quizzes or games do you actually like?
  • What makes something fun for you?
  • What makes something boring, confusing, or annoying?

Then write what you learned, pick a theme, sketch on paper, and upload both the sketch and the final quiz.
