2 · AI Talk: Permission And What Looks Real

Big idea: Today we are making a story about a real person. AI can help with words and images, but it does not know what is okay to share. Also, AI can make pictures that look real even when they are not real. If it looks real, be extra careful.

✏️

3 · Design Corner: Facts Become Stories

Today's design skill: listening for the feeling inside a fact. A fact is true information. A story is what we do with that information so another person feels something.

Kid phrase: Facts are ingredients. Story is the recipe.

Fact

Maya likes soccer.

Feeling

Fast, brave, part of a team.

Story line

Maya runs toward goals with bright, brave feet.
