2 · AI Talk: OCR And Trust

Big idea: AI can read a photo and organize messy information, but it can also misread dates, phone numbers, prices, and locations. For public-service information, unclear details must be checked by a human.

✏️

3 · Design Corner: Ask Before You Sort

Today's design skill: information architecture. Kid phrase: messy boards become helpful when we sort them for real people.

Ask the librarian only two questions. Use the answers to decide which notices matter most and which languages to prepare for next class.

Use these answers in your noticeboard. If the librarian says people often ask about printing, events, or library cards, look for notices that help with those needs.
📷

4 · Field Mission: Safe Photos

Take one noticeboard photo for ChatGPT. A simple photo is enough; the useful page matters more than the picture.

Best photo: one clear noticeboard section with readable posters. Optional cover: outside sign, entrance sign, empty shelves, or empty room sign. Do not include: faces, children, private papers, computer screens, library cards, or strangers. If unsure: skip the photo and write "Based on library noticeboard photo. Please verify."
🪄
