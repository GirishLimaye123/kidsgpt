2 · AI Talk

Big idea: AI is a prediction machine. It read a giant chunk of the internet and got scary-good at guessing what words come next. It's not magic and it's not a person — it's patterns. That's why it can be brilliant AND confidently wrong in the same sentence. The builder (you) is always the boss.

✏️

3 · Design Corner: Feedback

Today's design skill: feedback — how a player knows something happened. Tap the design you think is better. No reading ahead — trust your gut, then we'll see why.

Round 1 — the player just answered a question…

✅ B wins. Players need proof their action worked. Color + motion + a tiny reward = feedback. Without it, games feel broken even when they aren't.

Round 2 — the player got an answer WRONG…

✅ A wins. Feedback should inform, not punish. Show what happened (heart fades) and help them improve (the hint). Mean games get closed.

5 · Trust Check

AI wrote your game fast — now be the boss and check its work:

① Play every question. Is each "correct" answer actually correct?
② Did your two feedback effects show up? Right effect AND wrong effect?
③ Try to break it: click fast, click twice, refresh mid-game.
