2 · AI Talk: Bots Talk To People

Big idea: a chatbot is not just code. It is an experience. Builders decide how it should behave with real people.

3 · Design Corner: Manners For Machines

Today's design skill: bot etiquette. Kid phrase: decide your bot's manners before you let it talk to people.

Rule: give your bot a personality, not a fake identity. It can be playful, calm, cheerful, or formal. It should not pretend to be a real librarian, celebrity, or character.
🔐
